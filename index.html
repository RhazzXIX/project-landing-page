Digimon World!
You suddenly got summoned in the Digital world, and when you woke up, you need to save the Digimons with the help of your Digimon Partner!
Choose your Digimon!
Botamon is a Slime Digimon hatched from the green egg. On the surface of its slime-shaped body, it has grown thick, black fuzz.
Digivolution base path of Agumon and Gabumon.
Poyomon is a Slime Digimon hatched from the orange egg. It has a translucent body. It is a Digimon Baby that drifts through the "Net Ocean" like a jellyfish.
Digivolution base path of Patamon and Biyomon.
Punimon is a Slime Digimon hatched from the blue egg. It is a new kind of newborn Digimon. Its gelatinous red body is squishy, and there are three feeler-like things growing on its head.
Digivolution base path of Elecmon and Penguinmon.
Yuramon is a Seed Digimon hatched from the pink egg. Its whole body is covered in a substance with the appearance of long, soft hair. it seeks an environment suitable for its growth, and drifts lightly through the air, like a dandelion seed.
Divolution base path of Palmon and Beatmon.
